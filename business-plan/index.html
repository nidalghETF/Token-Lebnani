Business Plan

Fractional Real Estate for the Lebanese Diaspora — Compliant, Capital-Light, Retail-First

Version 1.2 — December 2025

1. Executive Summary

Token-Lebnani is a regulated digital platform enabling Lebanese property owners to fractionalize and tokenize their real estate assets for sale to global retail investors. We do not own, manage, or acquire any property. Instead, we act as a compliant bridge: facilitating SPV formation, token issuance, investor onboarding, and secondary trading — all while operating outside Lebanese banking and without balance sheet risk.

  • Target: Lebanese diaspora and international retail investors seeking USD-denominated exposure to Beirut real estate. Target diaspora markets: Canada, Australia, EU (non-US Persons), GCC, West Africa. Geographic restrictions apply per ADGM and SEC regulations.
  • Jurisdiction: ADGM-licensed VASP (via sandbox); Lebanese SPVs per asset.
  • Tech Stack: DigiShares Launch Plan (Year 1) → Standard Plan (Year 2+).
  • Funding: $456,000 total ($356,000 operational + $100,000 contingency).
  • Go-to-Market: Digital + GCC community events.
  • Liquidity: Connected to ADGM-licensed MTF (RealEstate.Exchange, Hubbis Digital Assets, or ADDX.io) by Year 2.

2. Problem & Solution

Problem

  • Lebanese property owners lack liquidity — forced to wait for single buyers in a collapsed market.
  • Diaspora investors want exposure to Lebanese real estate but face high entry barriers, title opacity, and no fractional options.
  • Existing solutions are either unregulated (informal fractional sales) or institutional-only (private placements).

Solution

Token-Lebnani offers:

  • ✅ For Owners: Sell in pieces, no single-buyer dependency.
  • ✅ For Investors: Low entry (<$1,000), transparent ownership, USD/stablecoin pricing.
  • ✅ For Regulators: Full ADGM compliance, no LBP exposure, retail-safe disclosures.

3. Business Model Updated

Role

  • Platform facilitator only — we never take asset ownership.
  • Property owner → transfers title to new Lebanese SPV → SPV shares tokenized → tokens sold to investors.

Revenue Streams

  1. Issuance Fee: 3% of tokenized value (paid by owner/SPV).
  2. Primary Market Fees: 2.5% buyer fee + 2.5% seller fee (applied during initial token sale). Added
  3. Secondary Trading Fee: 1.5% buyer + 1.5% seller per trade (once live on ADGM-licensed MTF).
Secondary Market Activation: Trading will commence only upon sandbox exit (Year 2) via partnership with an ADGM-licensed MTF. Options under evaluation: Real Estate Exchange (REX), Hubbis Digital Assets, or ADDX.io.

Cost Structure

  • OpEx: $117,000 (Year 2) — compliance officer, platform, custody, support.
  • Contingency: $100,000 for delays or pipeline gaps.

4. Technology Stack: Phased Approach

Year 1 – DigiShares Launch Plan (€3,000 one-time + €300/month)

  • ERC-1404 token standard (transfer-restricted, compliant)
  • Self-custody + optional Coinbase wallet integration
  • SumSub KYC/AML ($299/month + per-check fees)
  • Features: Token management, internal marketplace, e-signing, cap table
  • Limitations: No institutional custody, no white-label UI

Year 2 – DigiShares Standard Plan (€25,000 one-time + €850/month)

  • Upgrade to ERC-3643 (T-REX protocol with built-in compliance)
  • API integration with MTF partners for secondary trading
  • White-label UI and admin dashboard
  • Stripe fiat processing + DocuSign
Rationale: DigiShares provides 80% of required functionality at 5% of custom development cost ($4K/year vs $500K+), enabling capital-efficient sandbox validation. Added

5. Regulatory Strategy

ADGM Regulatory Sandbox (Year 1)

  • License Type: Regulatory Sandbox (temporary, non-licensed)
  • Activities Permitted: Primary token issuance, investor onboarding, KYC/AML
  • Activities Restricted: No secondary trading, max 100 investors per property, $5M AUM cap
  • Capital Required: $22,000 ($80,000 AED) - covers application ($10,000) + supervision ($12,000)
  • Retail Access: Permitted with enhanced risk disclosures and suitability assessments
  • Duration: 12-18 months, extendable based on FSRA assessment

Sandbox Exit → Category 3D Full License

  • Minimum Capital: $500,000 (deposited only upon exit confirmation)
  • Exit Criteria:
    • 12 months compliant operational history
    • Clean annual AML/CFT audit
    • Sustained transaction volume (minimum 5 properties tokenized)
    • Established compliance framework with MLRO
    • FSRA readiness assessment approval
  • Timeline: Exit application submitted Month 16-18, license granted Month 20-24

Compliance Officer

  • Function outsourced to ADGM-licensed compliance firm (e.g., Waystone, Maples Group)
  • Cost: $50,000/year (all-in: MLRO, reporting, monitoring)
  • Advantage: Cheaper than in-house, faster FSRA acceptance

Investor Communications

  • LinkedIn thought leadership campaigns targeting Lebanese diaspora professionals in GCC
  • Monthly video webinars hosted on platform dashboard
  • Personalized email sequences for high-net-worth prospects
  • No social media messaging apps used for investor communications to maintain ADGM audit trail

Lebanese Facility Management Contractor

  • Purpose: Handle all LBP-denominated expenses without Token-Lebnani banking exposure
  • Process: Token-Lebnani UAE sends USD to contractor → contractor converts to LBP locally → pays taxes, utilities, maintenance
  • Cost: 2-3% of property opex (borne by SPV, not platform)
  • Tax Liability: Zero for Token-Lebnani. Property taxes handled at SPV level by contractor.

9. Risks & Mitigation

Risk Mitigation
ADGM sandbox rejection Pre-submission engagement with FSRA; use regulatory consultant
Sandbox exit failure Pre-engage FSRA from Month 12; maintain clean AML record; demonstrate sustained volume
Title disputes in Lebanon Mandatory legal opinion per asset + owner indemnity clause
Low secondary liquidity Exclusive listing on ADGM-licensed MTF; investor education on exit timeline
Tech/platform downtime Use Polygon (low gas); DigiShares SLA; backup communication via email
Investor confusion Clear disclaimers everywhere: "We do not own assets — tokens = SPV shares only"
FX/banking risk Zero Lebanese banking — all flows via non-Lebanese USD accounts
